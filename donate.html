Your donation will enable us to continue offering specialized services that will reach more community members with severe mental illness.

Tax deductible donations can be made out to XDS - Farm at Penny Lane. Mail checks to XDS, PO Box 368, Carrboro, NC 27510.

You can also contribute funds to the farm by using Amazon Smile, selecting “XDS Inc.”

In-kind donations of new or gently-used items are always appreciated. The following items are currently needed at the farm.

  • Burlap bags and old 100% cotton clothing items for mulching our aisles
  • Clean, plastic and paper grocery bags to package our produce for clients and for customers at the farmers market
  • Picnic tables
  • Egg cartons — dozen or six-pack size
  • Plastic storage bins with lids

We are grateful for all contributions to our health and wellness initiative.
