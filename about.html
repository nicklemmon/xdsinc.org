Our Mission

Our Mission is in partnership with UNC Center for Excellence in community mental health to empower and assist people with multiple disabilities to live in the community of their choice, achieve personal responsibility, and gain an enhanced quality of life by providing an array of clinically appropriate, person-centered, and flexible treatment, rehabilitation, and support services.

Our programs

Our values

  1. Support individuals to make meaningful choices and to achieve their personal and family goals.
  2. Respect the dignity and diversity of those we serve.
  3. Serve individuals most in need.
  4. Assist individuals to secure and keep a home and a job
  5. Use natural supports, such as family, friends, neighbors, and community organizations.
